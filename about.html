Our Story

Founded with a passion for technology and a commitment to excellent service

From Humble Beginnings

TotalTech started as a small family business in 2010 with a simple goal: to provide honest, reliable printer repair services when most companies were focused on selling new equipment.

What began as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted name in printer services, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across the region.

Today, we combine years of experience with the latest technology and techniques to provide comprehensive printer solutions for homes and businesses of all sizes.
